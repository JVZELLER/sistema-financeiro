    new(amount, currency_code \\ :BRL)

    Creates a new Money type with amount and currency: Default currency is :BRL

    Examples: 

      iex> Money.new(5)
  %Money{amount: 500, currency: :BRL}
  iex> Money.new(5, :USD)
  %Money{amount: 500, currency: :USD}

  %Money{amount: 578, currency: :USD}
  iex> Money.new(5, "USD")
